Features

What We Offer

XPayFi combines AI, DeFi, and real-world asset tokenization to power a new era of programmable finance.

Here’s what makes it unique:

XPayFi Digital Identity System (DID 3.0)
Builds a trusted identity infrastructure for the Web3 era, supports multi-chain and cross-platform identity aggregation, and integrates payment data, social graphs, and on-chain behaviors into dynamic credit scores.
XPayFi Bank Card Tokenization
Replace sensitive bank card information with a unique digital token, which can be used safely in mobile payments, online shopping and other scenarios, improving payment efficiency while ensuring transaction security.
XPayFi Liquidity Pool
The core financial infrastructure of the ecosystem, which not only provides deep liquidity for instant exchange for the payment network, but also serves as the underlying asset reserve pool for consumer finance, connecting buyers and sellers of the RWA exchange, forming a closed value loop of "payment-transaction-financial management".
Consumer Supply Chain Finance
Through blockchain, consumption and production are seamlessly connected, and payment data drives credit circulation, realizing a closed-loop value cycle of "consumer credit-supply chain financing-ecological feedback".
RWA & Transactions
By aggregating payment data and consumer credit portraits, RWA is automatically tokenized based on programmable financial protocols to build a closed-loop channel from consumer behavior to real asset investment.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Have questions about how XPayFi works? Here are some quick answers to help you understand our platform, technology, and vision.
What is XPayFi?

XPayFi is an AI-powered programmable finance platform that connects traditional finance and decentralized finance (DeFi).

It allows users to unlock the Time Value of Money (TVM), tokenize and trade real-world assets (RWAs), and access instant liquidity.

The platform integrates AI-driven smart contracts, RWA tokenization, cross-border payment rails, and DeFi liquidity — enabling users to move value across traditional and Web3 systems seamlessly.

Through partnerships with global payment providers, XPayFi also enables spending tokenized and crypto assets in real-world scenarios, closing the loop between digital and fiat economies.

How does the Time Value of Money (TVM) work?

The Time Value of Money (TVM) is the core innovation of XPayFi. It allows users to obtain liquidity based on the projected future value of their assets.

Users deposit assets such as stablecoins, tokenized RWAs, gaming rewards, or ecosystem tokens into TVM smart contracts. In return, they receive XFI tokens or other liquid tokens they can use immediately.

At contract maturity, the underlying value is either redeemed or additional yield is provided to compensate liquidity providers and token holders.

This model enables better capital efficiency — users no longer need to wait for yield cycles or asset appreciation to utilize value. Instead, they unlock liquidity while retaining ownership and upside.

What types of assets can be tokenized on XPayFi?

XPayFi supports tokenization of a wide range of real-world assets (RWAs).

The initial focus includes:
- Commercial invoices and supply chain receivables
- Real estate assets
- Commodities (energy, raw materials, etc.)
- Off-chain credit instruments

Through tokenization, these assets become liquid, tradable, and composable in DeFi markets.

The platform includes mechanisms for compliance and verification of asset backing, and collaborates with asset originators to ensure integrity and real-world enforceability of tokenized assets.

How is AI used in XPayFi?

AI powers several key aspects of XPayFi:

Market making: AI agents optimize liquidity across DeFi pools and centralized venues, ensuring stable and efficient execution.

Risk management: AI models monitor TVM contracts, RWA collateral quality, and on-chain liquidity conditions to dynamically adjust risk parameters.

Compliance & fraud detection: AI tools scan transaction patterns and RWA provenance data to detect anomalies and enforce compliance rules.

Treasury optimization: AI is used to manage treasury allocations across stablecoins, tokenized assets, and yield opportunities to maximize returns for the XPayFi ecosystem.

How can I use my crypto for real-world payments?

XPayFi connects crypto assets to real-world spending through partnerships with major global payment networks.

Users can link their XPayFi wallet or Visa token to payment networks such as Visa, PayPal, Alipay, etc.

When making a purchase, XPayFi's backend automatically converts the selected crypto or tokenized asset into fiat currency at the point of sale, allowing for a seamless user experience. This allows users to use yield-based, tokenized, or otherwise locked assets in real-world commerce without the need for manual conversion steps.

XPayFi is building an ecosystem that merges crypto liquidity with traditional payments into a frictionless payment process.
